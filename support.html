Frequently asked questions
Where are my journal entries stored?

Your entries and preferences are stored locally on your device in DailySpark's app data. They are not synchronized to a Ceytro cloud account.

How do I back up my journal?

Open DailySpark settings and use Export to create a JSON backup. Keep the file in a safe location. Use Import when you want to restore a compatible backup.

Why am I not receiving reminders?

Check that notifications are enabled for DailySpark in Android settings. Battery optimization and exact alarm restrictions may also affect scheduled reminders on some devices.

Why can I not add a photo?

DailySpark needs camera permission to take a new photo and photo or media permission to select one from your device. Enable the relevant permission in Android settings.

How do I protect my journal?

Use DailySpark's local access controls and keep your Android screen lock enabled. Do not share exported backup files with people you do not trust.

Why do I see advertisements?

DailySpark is supported by advertising. Ads are delivered through Google Mobile Ads. Availability and personalization can vary based on device, region and consent settings.

What should I do before reinstalling the app?

Export a backup first. Uninstalling the app or clearing its storage may remove locally stored journal data.

Still need help?

Tell us what happened, what you expected and which Android device or version you use. Screenshots are helpful, but remove any private journal text first.
